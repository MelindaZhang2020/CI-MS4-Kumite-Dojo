Adult and Children Karate Classes

Karate means "empty hand" in Japanese and anybody from all ages can master kicks, punches and strikes in this all round system. As a matter of fact, you can easily get your entire family involved with Karate. If you are curious about what would be involved in a typical Karate class, this article will give you a basic introduction.

Adult and Children bojutsu Classes

Bōjutsu, translated from Japanese as "staff technique", is the martial art of stick fighting using a bō, which is the Japanese word for staff.Some techniques involve slashing, swinging, and stabbing with the staff. Bo training is not only fun, it improves coordination, builds strength in the upper body, is low impact and gives you an understanding of weapon movement.

Tai-chi Classes

Tai chi is an internal Chinese martial art practiced for defense training, health benefits, it is practiced for reasons including competitive wrestling, competitive demonstrations, and health/longevity.and meditation.

Fighting Fit Classes

Fighting Fit is ideal for anyone keen to learn proper boxing techniques while getting fit. This class is a full body workout incorporating weight training, cardiovascular and resistance exercises to help you build a fighter’s endurance, an iron core, and shed any excess fat.

Tiger tots Classes

Kumite Dojo offers the best Kids Karate Classes to kids age 5 onwards. They are a great way for children to improve their concentration, agility, balance, coordination, fitness and confidence. In also a great environment for them to make friends, learn to be more respectful and most importantly, have fun!

Kicboxing Classes

Kickboxing is a group of stand-up combat sports based on kicking and punching, historically developed from karate mixed with boxing. Kickboxing is practiced for self-defence, general fitness, or as a contact sport.
